    Expo 2020 Dubai

    The first Expo in the 170-year history of the World Expo to be held in the Middle East, Africa, and South Asia region, Expo 2020 Dubai is a transformative global gathering that celebrates humanity.




    What is the Purpose of an Expo?

    An expo’s goal is to bring countries together for collaborative initiatives that help to accelerate and promote ideas that enhance people’s lives while also conserving the environment. Moreover, an expo attracts tens of millions of people, providing opportunities for countries to erect spectacular pavilions, and alter the host city for years.




    Impact of COVID-19 on Dubai Expo 2020

    Dubai Expo 2020, initially scheduled for October, was postponed for a year due to the pandemic. The BIE General Assembly agreed to the delay at the UAE government's request, aiming to ensure the event reflects current realities.

    An Unforgettable Trip with Friends

    This was my first time traveling to Dubai alone with my friends, and the experience was incredible! It truly was an amazing experience, and the UAE was the perfect host for the Expo!
